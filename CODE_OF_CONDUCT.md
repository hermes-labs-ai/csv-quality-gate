# Code of Conduct

This project follows a simple professional conduct standard:

- be respectful
- assume good faith
- discuss behavior and code, not people
- avoid harassment, insults, or intimidation

Unacceptable behavior includes:

- abusive language
- discriminatory conduct
- intimidation
- doxxing
- bad-faith disruption

Maintainer actions may include warning, moderation, or removal from the project space.
